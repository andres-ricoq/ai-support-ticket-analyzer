# Support Policies

## Technical Support Team

Responsible for resolving technical issues affecting the customer experience.

Handles:

* Hardware failures
* Software bugs
* Network and connectivity issues
* Application crashes
* Login and authentication errors
* Product malfunctions

Escalation Criteria:

* Customer unable to access the service
* Critical system outage
* Repeated technical failures

---

## Billing Team

Responsible for payment and subscription-related matters.

Handles:

* Billing inquiries
* Subscription management
* Failed payments
* Invoice requests
* Payment disputes

Escalation Criteria:

* Payment processed incorrectly
* Subscription incorrectly canceled
* Repeated billing failures

---

## Refund Team

Responsible for refund requests and financial claims.

Handles:

* Refund requests
* Charge disputes
* Incorrect charges
* Duplicate payments

Escalation Criteria:

* High-value transactions
* Legal or regulatory complaints

---

## Customer Support Team

Responsible for general customer assistance.

Handles:

* Product information
* Usage questions
* Account guidance
* General inquiries

Escalation Criteria:

* Customer dissatisfaction
* Multiple unresolved contacts

---

# Priority Rules

## High Priority

Criteria:

* Customer cannot use the product
* Account is blocked
* Payment failure preventing service access
* Critical functionality unavailable

Target Response Time:

* Within 4 hours

---

## Medium Priority

Criteria:

* Partial functionality affected
* Workaround available
* Non-critical feature malfunction

Target Response Time:

* Within 24 hours

---

## Low Priority

Criteria:

* General information requests
* Product questions
* Feature guidance

Target Response Time:

* Within 48 hours

---

# Service Level Agreements (SLA)

| Priority | First Response |
| -------- | -------------- |
| High     | 4 Hours        |
| Medium   | 24 Hours       |
| Low      | 48 Hours       |
